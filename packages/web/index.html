Stop trusting 4.7★.

TrueScore reads every Google review on a place, drops the one-time tourists and lukewarm 3-stars, and gives you the only number that matters: do regulars actually love it.

Open Maps, find a place, hit Share → Copy link, paste above. Works with maps.app.goo.gl and full URLs.

YOUR PLACES
METHOD
  1. 01Pull every review from Most relevant and Newest.
  2. 02Drop reviewers with fewer than 3 reviews. They haven’t calibrated.
  3. 03Count 5★ as +1, 1★ as −1, ignore the in-betweens.
  4. 04Score 0 to 100. Above 60 trusted. Below 30, walk.
