## Findings considered and rejected

- **"Score-store divides `timestamp` by 1000 — off by 1000×"**: NOT a bug. The
  store receives Google timestamps in **microseconds** by contract
  (`packages/extension/src/shared/score-store.test.ts:7-9` documents this; the
  `/1000` converts to ms), and the period-bucketing test passes. Do not "fix" it.
- **"`scorePct` returns NaN on zero trusted reviews"**: NOT a bug.
  `score-store.ts:151` is `a / b || 0`; `NaN` is falsy, so `NaN || 0 === 0`.
- **Split `gmaps.ts` (1522 lines) into modules**: rejected by `docs/adr/0002` —
  the deletion test fails, the complexity is inherent to one content script.
- **Batch per-chip highlight RPCs into one Google request** (`highlights.ts`):
  needs a reverse-engineering spike on Google's undocumented RPC first; HIGH risk
  of silent breakage, MED confidence it's even possible. Spike, don't build.
- **Code-split shared/ out of the 21 esbuild bundles**: the duplicated bytes are
  well under the MV3 size budget; readability cost outweighs the saving.
